📘 Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)

About the Course

The Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) is a three-year undergraduate degree program designed to provide students with a strong foundation in commerce, accounting, finance, taxation, economics, and business management.

It prepares students for careers in the corporate sector, banking, insurance, government services, and entrepreneurship.

Duration

📅 3 Years (6 Semesters) – Full Time

Eligibility

  • ✅ 10+2 (Senior Secondary) or equivalent examination from a recognized board with Commerce/Mathematics/Economics as a subject.
  • ✅ Minimum percentage as per University/College norms.

Course Highlights

  • Financial Accounting & Corporate Accounting
  • Business Economics & Business Law
  • Cost Accounting & Management Accounting
  • Taxation (Direct & Indirect)
  • Auditing
  • Business Communication & Management Principles
  • Computer Applications in Business
  • Entrepreneurship Development

Career Opportunities

After completing B.Com, students can pursue:

  • Chartered Accountancy (CA)
  • Company Secretary (CS)
  • Cost & Management Accountancy (CMA)
  • Banking and Financial Services
  • MBA (Finance/Marketing/HR)
  • Government Exams (UPSC, SSC, Banking, Insurance)
  • Entrepreneurship

Facilities Provided

  • 🏫 Well-equipped classrooms
  • 📚 Library with commerce & finance journals
  • 💻 Computer labs for practical learning
  • 👨‍🏫 Experienced faculty
  • 💼 Internship & Placement support

🎓 Bachelor of Arts (B.A)

About the Course

The Bachelor of Arts (B.A) is a three-year undergraduate degree program in the field of Humanities and Social Sciences. It helps students develop strong communication, analytical, and critical thinking skills.

The program offers a wide choice of subjects such as English, Hindi, Punjabi, History, Political Science, Economics, Sociology, Psychology, Philosophy, and Public Administration.

Duration

📅 3 Years (6 Semesters) – Full Time

Eligibility

  • ✅ 10+2 (Senior Secondary) or equivalent examination from a recognized board in any stream.
  • ✅ Minimum percentage as per University/College rules.

Course Highlights

  • Language & Literature (English, Hindi, Punjabi)
  • History & Culture of India
  • Political Science & Public Administration
  • Sociology & Psychology
  • Economics & Philosophy
  • Environmental Studies (as per UGC guidelines)
  • Skill-based and Elective subjects

Career Opportunities

  • Teaching & Research (after higher studies like M.A, NET, Ph.D.)
  • Civil Services & Government Jobs (UPSC, State PCS, SSC, etc.)
  • Journalism & Mass Communication
  • Law (LL.B)
  • Social Work & NGOs
  • Corporate Sector (HR, Public Relations, Communication roles)

Facilities Provided

  • 🏫 Modern classrooms
  • 📚 Well-stocked library
  • 👨‍🏫 Qualified & experienced faculty
  • 💻 Computer & digital resources
  • 🎭 Co-curricular & cultural activities

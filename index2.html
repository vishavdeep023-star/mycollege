About Shaheed Udham Singh College

Established in 2011, PUCCGHS stands as a beacon of quality education in rural Punjab. Located in Village Mohan Ke Hithar, Tehsil Guru Har Sahai, District Ferozepur, our college operates in collaboration with Panjab University, Punjab Government, and UGC.

We are committed to providing value-based education that nurtures not just academic excellence but also character development and social responsibility.

  • UGC Recognized Institution
  • Affiliated with Panjab University
  • Experienced Faculty
  • Modern Infrastructure
  • Holistic Development Programs
  • Green & Social Initiatives

🎓 Post Graduate Diploma in Computer Applications (pgdca)

About the Course

The Post Graduate Diploma in Computer Applications (pgdca) is a one-year undergraduate program focused on computer science and applications. It prepares students for careers in software development, IT, data science, and related fields.

Duration

📅 1 Years (2 Semesters) – Full Time

Eligibility

  • ✅ 10+2 (Senior Secondary) with Mathematics/Computer Science preferred.
  • ✅ Minimum percentage as per University/College rules.

Subjects Covered

  1. Computer Fundamentals & IT
  2. Programming in C, C++, Java, Python
  3. Data Structures
  4. Database Management Systems (DBMS)
  5. Web Development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP)
  6. Operating Systems
  7. Computer Networks
  8. Software Engineering
  9. Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (Basics)
  10. Data Science (Introduction)

Career Opportunities

  • Software Developer
  • Web & Mobile App Developer
  • Data Analyst
  • Network & System Administrator
  • Cyber Security Analyst
  • AI & ML Engineer (with higher studies)

Higher Studies Options

  • MCA (Master of Computer Applications)
  • MBA (IT/Management)
  • M.Sc. in Computer Science
  • Specialized Certifications (Cloud, Data Science, AI)

Facilities Provided

  • 🏫 Modern computer labs with internet access
  • 📚 Updated syllabus and study material
  • 👨‍🏫 Experienced faculty
  • 💻 Internship & project opportunities
  • 🎭 Cultural & co-curricular activities
